About College

Saket College of Arts, Science & Commerce (Affiliated to University of Mumbai) was established in 2002 and offers courses leading to B.A., B.Com., B.M.S., B.Com.(B & I), B. Com (A & F), B.Com.(IM), B.Com.(FM), B.Sc.(I.T.), B.Sc (C.S.), M.Sc.(I.T.), M.Com. (Advanced Accountancy), and M.A.(Hindi).

With over 5,000 students, our institution is renowned for campus discipline, high academic standards, and comprehensive personality development. We also provide ample opportunities in extracurricular and co-curricular activities including sports, cultural programs, NSS, and DLLE.
